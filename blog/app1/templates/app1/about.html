About MyBlog

Welcome to MyBlog! This is a platform where users can create, edit, and share their blog posts. Whether you are writing about tech, travel, or your personal experiences, MyBlog provides a clean and simple interface to make your content shine.

This project is built using Django for the backend and Tailwind CSS for styling. It demonstrates real-world CRUD functionality, including:

  • Creating, editing, and deleting posts
  • Uploading images with posts
  • User authentication and author management
  • Responsive and modern design using Tailwind

MyBlog is a learning project to practice Django, Tailwind, and general web development best practices. Feel free to explore the posts and create your own!
